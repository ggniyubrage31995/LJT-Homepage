On the Universal Truthfulness Hyperplane Inside LLMs

## Abstract

We discover a universal truthfulness hyperplane inside large language models that can distinguish between truthful and untruthful statements. Our findings provide new insights into the internal mechanisms of LLMs and offer a simple yet effective method for improving model truthfulness.

## Keywords

Large Language Models, Truthfulness, Interpretability, Model Analysis
