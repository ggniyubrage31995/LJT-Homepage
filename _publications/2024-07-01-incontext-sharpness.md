In-Context Sharpness as Alerts: An Inner Representation Perspective for Hallucination Mitigation

## Abstract

We propose a novel approach for mitigating hallucinations in large language models by leveraging in-context sharpness as an alert mechanism. Our method analyzes the inner representations of models to detect potential hallucinations before they are generated.

## Keywords

Hallucination Mitigation, Large Language Models, In-Context Learning, Representation Analysis
