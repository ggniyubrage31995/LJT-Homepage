Synthesizing Verifiable Reasoning Data at Scale for Learning Logical Reasoning and Beyond

## Abstract

We present SynLogic, a novel approach for synthesizing verifiable reasoning data at scale for learning logical reasoning. Our method generates high-quality reasoning traces that can be automatically verified, enabling efficient training of language models on complex reasoning tasks.

## Keywords

Logical Reasoning, Data Synthesis, Large Language Models, Reasoning Data
