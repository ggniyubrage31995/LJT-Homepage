On the Perception Bottleneck of VLMs for Chart Understanding

## Abstract

We investigate the perception bottleneck of vision-language models (VLMs) when applied to chart understanding tasks. Our analysis reveals key limitations in current VLMs and proposes improvements for better chart comprehension.

## Keywords

Vision-Language Models, Chart Understanding, Perception, Multimodal Learning
