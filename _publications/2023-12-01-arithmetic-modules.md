Composing Parameter-Efficient Modules with Arithmetic Operations

## Abstract

We investigate the composition of parameter-efficient modules using arithmetic operations, enabling flexible and efficient model adaptation without full fine-tuning.

## Keywords

Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning, Model Composition, Arithmetic Operations, Efficient Adaptation
