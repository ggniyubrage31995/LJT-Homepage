C-Eval: A Multi-Level Multi-Discipline Chinese Evaluation Suite for Foundation Models

## Abstract

We present C-Eval, a comprehensive evaluation suite for testing the knowledge and reasoning abilities of foundation models in Chinese. C-Eval covers a wide range of subjects across different difficulty levels, providing a thorough assessment of model capabilities.

## Keywords

Evaluation Benchmark, Foundation Models, Chinese NLP, Multi-Discipline Evaluation
